INTRO

Maintain professionalism from your own home.

At the crucial moment of that meeting, a family member bursts in and ruins the call. Now, as the current situation forces us to work from home, we’re here to help, providing you with the ultimate key to a much more professional environment right in your own home. The Do Not Deskturb, a fully integrated remotely operated conspicuously lit ‘Do Not Disturb’ sign, gives your working world the boundaries it desperately needs.
