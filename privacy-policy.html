Private Data We Receive And Collect

Firefly automatically collects and receives certain information from your computer or mobile device, the type of hardware and software you are using (for example, your operating system or browser), and information obtained from cookies. For example, each time you visit the Website or otherwise use the Services, we automatically collect your IP address, browser and device type and access times.

When you first register for a Firefly newletter, and when you use the Services, we collect some personal information about you.

  • The geographic area where you use your computer and mobile devices
  • Your full name, phone number (if applicable), and email address and other contact details
  • View our cookie policy
  • Your IP Address and, when applicable, timestamp related to your consent and confirmation of consent
  • Information (such as messages, profile, comments etc) we may receive relating to communications you send us, or when you interact with us on social media
